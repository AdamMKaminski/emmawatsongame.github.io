how to play
-------------------------------------------------
1. Player A determines the starting and ending nouns for player B

2. Player B presses "play" to begin

3. Player B makes a series of semantic or associative leaps from the starting noun to the ending noun

EXAMPLE: If the starting noun is apple and the ending noun is astrophysics, player B could go: apple ... apple tree ... newton ... physics ... astrophysics

NOTES: To make a leap, enter one word into the box left of "submit" / Press "submit" to submit / Try to use all lowercase

4. Player A reviews the semantic or associative leaps of player B. Was there... cheating?

5. Player B enters their ~moves score~ and their ~time score~ into the stats section

6. Switch the roles of player A and player B. Repeat steps 1 through 5

7. Repeat steps 1 through 6 as many times as you darn well please

8. At some point, compute the scores to determine a winner
